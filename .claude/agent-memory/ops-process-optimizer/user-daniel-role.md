Daniel Galindo is the owner/operator of Vault 1040 (vault1040.com), a tax preparation firm. He manages a small but specialized team:

- **Daniel** — Owner/final reviewer. Handles audits, collections, appeals, business notices, high-dollar assessments, and strategic decisions. Time must be protected — only escalate matters requiring owner authority or expertise.
- **Genesis** — Lead tax preparer, first technical reviewer. Owns the tax research and response-drafting workflow.
- **Javier** — Administrative intake. Receives, scans, uploads documents, creates records, handles submission/mailing.
- **Scarlett** — Client communication. Confirms receipt, requests documents, follows up with clients.
- **Evelina** — Workflow coordinator. Monitors deadlines, tracks aging items, escalates urgencies, keeps Daniel informed.

Daniel is the primary developer and user of this dashboard. He has a strong grasp of the business domain and has been building out this system feature by feature.
